ResNet PGD accuracy: 95.82%, 75.52%, 2.76%, 0.17%, 0.00%, 0.01%. ViT PGD accuracy: 97.80%, 55.18%, 8.69%, 2.78%, 1.12%, 0.58%. Human accuracy at 0.00, 0.05, 0.10, 0.20, 0.30: 73.33%, 69.17%, 59.17%, 62.22%, 58.61%.
The two-regime finding: At ε=0.01, ResNet outperforms ViT (75.5% vs 55.2%) because local convolutions smooth imperceptible noise. At ε≥0.05, ViT outperforms ResNet (8.7% vs 2.8%) because global attention integrates uncorrupted spatial patches. The human-CNN divergence point at ε=0.05 shows a 66.3% accuracy gap — the project's headline result.
